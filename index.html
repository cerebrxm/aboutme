About Me

Research Focus

I am a GMC-licensed medical professional and public health researcher dedicated to improving health outcomes through rigorous evidence synthesis and programme evaluation. My work spans malaria control, immunisation, maternal and child health, tobacco harm reduction, and infectious disease surveillance.

Previously, I served at Nigeria's National Primary Health Care Development Agency, where I led national-level monitoring and evaluation programmes, designed quality improvement protocols across 18 states, and contributed to policy formulation through data-driven insights.

  • Evidence synthesis & systematic review
  • Epidemiological research & surveillance
  • Cost-effectiveness analysis & modelling
  • Monitoring & evaluation framework design
  • Quantitative analysis (R, SPSS, Power BI)
  • Policy analysis & stakeholder engagement
  • Programme evaluation (LQAS, PAPA)
  • Research governance & ethics (GCP certified)
